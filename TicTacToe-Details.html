Multiplayer TicTacToe with various Gamemodes and AI

Project information

  • Category: Unity
  • Genre: Party Game
  • Project date: April, 2023

A Unity remake of the classic TicTacToe pen and paper game.

The game works just like the classic, whereby players take it in turns to try and get 3 symbols in a row. This version includes 2 skill-levels of AI to play against, as well as a local multiplayer option.
The game also includes 2 other gamemodes to deviate from the classic ruleset: A speed mode where each player only gets 2 seconds to make their move; as well as a hidden mode, where the icons are hidden, and memory becomes important.
