NPC.GiveQuest() is a game about the often forgotten heroes of the RPG world - the NPC's. It's up to you to forge the path and curate adventures for our brave new heroes. So be creative! But you best keep up your A-game, because I hear the developer is ready to make cuts.

Your goal is to make it through a new RPG's beta week, serving as a Quest Giver for the many lands. Build quests by dragging cards with left click and releasing them over the adventurer. Submit the quest to confirm! Your reputation will change based on the quality of your quests, so stay vigilant or you might just get patched out.

Pay attention to the end of day patch notes for potential new rules or stipulations you'll need to follow.

You can access the pause menu by pressing Esc.
