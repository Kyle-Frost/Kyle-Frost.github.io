About Me

My name is Kyle Frost, I spent 4 years as a Games Technology student at the University of the West of England (UWE). Throughout my studies, I have developed strong Unity/C# skills through various game developments as well as systems creations. I have also developed skills whithin C++ and gained experience with DirectX as well as a custom ASGE engine. I have also amassed knowledge of core systems and fundamentals for games in my Advanced Technologies module.

I have a strong interest in not only games, but how they work. I also enjoy the communities that surround games and have regularly taken part in eSport teams and events during my time at University. Development communities have been fun to be a part of, and my participation in game jams have allowed me to connect with others in a fun and helpful way.
