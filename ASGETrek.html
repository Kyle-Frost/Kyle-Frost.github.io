ASGE Trek

ASGE Trek is a C++ remake of the classic EGA Trek using the UWE Awesome Sauce Gamer Engine (ASGE).

Akin to the original, ASGETrek use text input to input commands to your ship, the U.S.S Lexington. The objective of the game is to travel around the 64-quadrant galaxy, searching for and eliminating all Mongol threats. The quadrant grid displays how many Mongol ships reside in each quadrant, and reveal based on distance, requiring the player to travel and explore. The sector grid displays all spacecraft and obstacles in your current sector, allowing the player to move around, dock with star bases, and fight enemies.

The Lexington is armed with combat lasers and torpedos to take down enemy spacecraft. Torpedos deal the most damage, but have a higher chance of missing; whilst lasers are more accurate but drain the ships power depending on how much charge is provided. Distance to enemies affects accuracy, so correct movment and decision making is essential for success in combat. Using the scan command will reutrn information about all enemy ships in the area, allowing for more strategic attack strategies.

Gameplay information is displayed using a dynamic notification area. Information about combat and movement will be displayed in the bottom right, and updated accordingly as the players explores the galaxy. The game offers various playstyles - those who prefer destruction can speed through the galaxy, seeking out enemies, only ever stopping to resupply; or those who prefer to act methodically can pick their battles and plan for repairs.
